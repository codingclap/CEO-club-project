Who We Are

CEO Clubs India is an exclusive business network that brings together CEOs, entrepreneurs, and business leaders from various industries to share knowledge, ideas, and resources. It is a part of the global CEO Clubs Worldwide, which has a presence in more than 10 countries.

Value of Membership

Inner Circles

At the heart of the CEO CLUB, is the Inner- Circle, which comprises 10-12 members (representing non-competing business interests) and meets once a month. The Inner Circle provides members a platform to learn through sharing experiences, bonding in an atmosphere of utmost confidentiality, trust, and respect.

Knowledge & Leadership Series

These events are an excellent platform for members to gain valuable insights from eminent speakers who have achieved great success in various walks of life. Members can expand their knowledge, learn new skills, and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices by attending these events.

Business Series Events

These events are a highly beneficial resource for members who want to enhance their professional journey. They offer a diverse range of topics related to business, such as different models, strategies, and practices. Members can learn from experts in their fields and get insights into the challenges and opportunities they may face in their own organizations.

Social Series Events

These events are a fun and engaging way for members and their families to build camaraderie and support within the club. By rotating the hosting of these events, members can get to know each other better and celebrate their fellow members' unique talents, traditions, and cultures.

Chapter Retreat

The annual retreat for all members and their families of each chapter is intended to make exploration enjoyable and strengthen bonding.

Annual National Conference - Go Beyond

Go Beyond Conference is held once a year and is exclusively addressed to CEO Clubs members, their family, leadership teams, and guest C-suite executives. This is a time to introspect, engage, and learn together to inspire each other and Go Beyond.
