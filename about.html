About Us

CEO Clubs India is a not-for-profit organisation that provides a platform for CEOs, entrepreneurs, innovators, and leaders from all walks of life to connect, collaborate, and grow. Launched in March 2008 in Hyderabad, India, under the umbrella of CEO Clubs International, established in the USA in 1977, we have since expanded to have chapters in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Pune, Delhi, and Ahmedabad.

As part of CEO Clubs International, which now operates in 10 countries, we offer our members a global network of like-minded individuals, opening doors to new opportunities for growth and success. This is just the beginning, and we have many more miles to go, friends to make, and journeys to traverse together. Join us and become a part of our vibrant community of business leaders.

Our Founders

Joe Mancuso

Founder, CEO Clubs International

Joe Mancuso is a distinguished entrepreneur and a highly esteemed speaker. The talks and workshops he has conducted or arranged have attracted a million attendees, generating over $300,000,000 in value. He started the CEO Clubs in 1977, and today the Club has chapters in China, India, Dubai, Greece, the Philippines, Australia, and Fiji.

Hari K. Vallurupalli

Founder, CEO Clubs India

Hari is an esteemed leader who brought CEO Clubs International to India in 2008 with the aim of bringing the best minds of our country together (regardless of age, gender or any boundaries. Today, the Club has chapters in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Pune, Delhi and Ahmedabad.
